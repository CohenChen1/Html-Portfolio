The Best Movie According to Chen

My top 3 movies of all-time

Gold

"Gold" is a riveting film featuring Matthew McConaughey's captivating performance, exploring the high-stakes world of mining and the complexities of the American Dream.

Couch Carter

"Coach Carter" is an inspirational sports drama anchored by Samuel L. Jackson's powerful portrayal of a high school basketball coach who prioritizes discipline, education, and character development.

Black Hawk Down

Ridley Scott's "Black Hawk Down" is an intense and realistic war drama that vividly portrays the chaos, bravery, and sacrifice of American soldiers during the Battle of Mogadishu in Somalia.
